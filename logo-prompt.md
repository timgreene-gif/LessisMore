# Subtraction Health Logo Prompt

## Brand Context
- **Name:** Subtraction Health
- **Tagline:** "Stop Taking. Start Removing."
- **Philosophy:** A health approach focused on removing harmful factors (medications, habits, toxins) rather than adding supplements
- **Target Audience:** Health-conscious adults experiencing fatigue, brain fog, or sleep issues

---

## Logo Generation Prompt

### Primary Prompt (Recommended)

```
Minimalist logo for "Subtraction Health", a wellness brand. Design features a stylized minus sign (-)
elegantly integrated with a leaf or organic element, symbolizing the philosophy of "removing to heal."
Color palette: deep teal (#1a5f5f) as primary with coral accent (#e85a4f). Clean, modern sans-serif
typography. The minus symbol should feel like growth/wellness, not negative. White background,
vector-style, suitable for app icon and web use. Professional health brand aesthetic.
```

### Alternative Prompts

**Option A - Abstract Mark:**
```
Modern abstract logo mark for health tech brand "Subtraction Health". Circular or rounded design
incorporating a subtle minus/subtraction symbol with organic flowing lines suggesting wellness and
vitality. Deep teal (#1a5f5f) primary color. Minimal, scalable, works as app icon. Clean geometric
shapes with soft edges. Premium SaaS health app aesthetic.
```

**Option B - Lettermark:**
```
Lettermark logo using "SH" initials for "Subtraction Health". The letters incorporate a clever
negative space minus sign. Deep teal (#1a5f5f) color. Modern, geometric sans-serif style.
Minimalist design suitable for favicon and app icon. Health and wellness brand, professional
and trustworthy feeling.
```

**Option C - Symbol + Wordmark:**
```
Logo design for "Subtraction Health" featuring a custom icon of a leaf with a minus sign cut out
as negative space, paired with clean modern wordmark. Teal (#1a5f5f) and white color scheme.
The design communicates natural health through removal/subtraction. Flat design, vector style,
suitable for digital and print. Professional healthcare brand aesthetic.
```

**Option D - Minimal Icon:**
```
App icon logo for health tracking app. A circle with a stylized minus sign that subtly forms a
horizon or landscape, suggesting balance and wellness. Deep teal gradient (#1a5f5f to #2d8a8a).
Minimal flat design. Modern health tech aesthetic. Should work at 512x512 and 32x32 sizes.
```

---

## Logo Requirements

- **Formats needed:** SVG, PNG (transparent), favicon
- **Sizes:** 512x512 (app icon), 192x192, 32x32 (favicon)
- **Variations:** Full color, monochrome, dark mode variant
- **Style:** Clean, modern, trustworthy, professional healthcare
- **Must work:** As standalone icon AND with wordmark

---

## Key Symbolism to Capture

1. **Subtraction/Minus** - The core concept of removing rather than adding
2. **Health/Wellness** - Organic, natural, vitality
3. **Simplicity** - Less is more philosophy
4. **Trust** - Professional, medical-adjacent credibility
5. **Modern Tech** - Digital-first, app-focused design
